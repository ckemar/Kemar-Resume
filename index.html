Summary Statement

Kemar Celestin is an Engineer with a strong software development and project management background. He has thirteen years of experience working on HIV/AIDS data management, Monitoring and Evaluation, health information exchange, software development, and software implementation/management. He started as a Health Management Information System (HMIS) Officer at I-TECH/CHARESS and serves as Director of Information Systems currently. Kemar has excellent training skills, a high level of initiative, and creativity. He holds a master’s degree in computer science and technology from The University of the West Indies, St Augustine, Trinidad & Tobago, and a Bac+5 in Electronic Engineering from the State University of Haiti. He is fluent in French, Haitian Kreyol, and English.
